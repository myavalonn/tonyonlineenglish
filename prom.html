晉升英語面試：深度需求挖掘表

Promotion Interview Deep Dive Questionnaire

這份問卷將做為我們 4 月份口說特訓的基礎，請以「決策者」的角度思考並詳細填寫。
Please fill out this form from a "decision-maker's" perspective to help us structure your career narrative and customize your training.

00 基本資訊 / Background Info
01 個人介紹與定位 / Personal Intro & Positioning
請簡短描述你的核心競爭力（你是誰？最擅長解決什麼問題？） Briefly describe your core competency. Who are you and what problems do you solve best?
請列出新職位中最重要的三個關鍵績效指標 (KPI)。 Please list the top 3 Key Performance Indicators (KPIs) expected in the new role.
過去半年內，發生了什麼關鍵事件讓你認為自己「準備好了」？ What key events in the past 6 months have proven that you are "ready" for this level?
02 工作績效與影響力 / Performance & Impact
請提供一個你利用專業能力，直接為公司節省預算或增加營收的案例。(包含具體數字與對比) Provide an example of how you saved budget or generated revenue. (Include specific numbers/metrics)
描述一次你「主動」發現問題並解決，而非只是等待指令的經驗。 Describe a time you proactively identified and solved a problem without being asked.
03 未來規劃與挑戰 / Future Plans & Challenges
如果你成功晉升，你打算解決的第一個問題是什麼？有什麼具體行動？ If promoted, what is the very first problem you will tackle? What are your concrete action items?
上任後，你將如何說服「不同部門」或「高層主管」支持你的提案？ Describe how you will manage cross-functional teams and senior management in your new role.
如果你是面試官，你會質疑目前的自己哪個部分還不夠資深？打算如何回應？ If you were the interviewer, what gap or weakness would you challenge yourself on? How will you address it?
